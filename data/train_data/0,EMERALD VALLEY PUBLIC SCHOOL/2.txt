', Emerald Valley Public School (E.V.P.S)', [', edit', ]', 
', Emerald Valley Public School',  is situated on ', Yercaud',  foothills at ', Salem', , ', Tamil Nad, , India. It is based on ', Central Board of Secondary Education',  (CBSE). The correspondent of the school is Mrs.Meenaakshi Sethu. More than 2000 students study here. It is a co education school. This school provides special coaching for differently abled children. There was a grand festival named "SUTRAM 2013". Around 100 schools from Salem were invited. Namalwar Ayya, Mr Gopinath, Dr. Sivaraman and many environmentalists were honoured. It was a two-day show. It was accompanied with dinner, only made of millets and grains. On 21.02.2014 there was a garbage segregating awareness program at the school auditorium. Mr.Piyush gave a brief introduction about the issue.All concerned government officials were also present. People from Kupamedu-Chetisavadi came and spoke about the problems they face due to these garbage. F The students and the teachers deliberated on ways to reduce the garbage, segregation and to stop using plastics. They have also played a major role in the revival of many water bodies in Salem - like Mookenari, Ammapet Kumaragiri Lake, Chatram theppakkulam and te pallappatti theppakkulam. .',
