', Muthoot Pappachan Group',  (MPG), founded in 1887, is an Indian business house headquartered in ', Kochi', , ', Kerala', , ', India', . It encompasses several businesses, including ', financial services', , automotive dealerships, ', hospitality', , ', real estate', , ', entertainment', , and ', health services', .', 
', Businesses', 
', 
', Muthoot Capital Services was listed on the ', Bombay Stock Exchange',  and offers loans for the purchase of vehicles. The company is licensed by RBI to accept deposits and offers deposit products. MCSL is active in retail financing and works with borrowers in rural and semi-urban areas.', [1]', 
', Muthoot Housing Finance Company Ltd caters mainly to the housing finance requirements of customers in the middle and lower income category.', [2]',  It has a division called Muthoot MahilaMitra which offers microloans to women.', [3]', 
', Muthoot Gold Point in Coimbatore buys scrap, old and used gold items directly from end customers.', [4]', 
', Muthoot Risk Insurance and Broking Services Pvt. Ltd offers various forms of insurance.', [5]', 
', u"Muthoot Pappachan Technologies (P) Ltd. provides IT support to the group's companies.", [6]', 
', The automotive division sells cars across Calicut, Ernakulam, Kollam and Trivandrum districts.', [7]', 
', The group has a hospitality division that owns hotels and provides catering services to airlines.', [8]', [9]', [10]', 
', 
', In 2014 the group began sponsoring the ', Kerala Blasters FC',  football club.', [11]',
