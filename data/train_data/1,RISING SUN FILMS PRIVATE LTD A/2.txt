', Shoojit Sircar',  (', Bengali', : ', \u09b8\u09c1\u099c\u09bf\u09a4 \u09b8\u09b0\u0995\u09be\u09b0', ) is an ', Indian',  ', film director',  and ', producer', . He directed ', Yahaan',  (2005), ', Vicky Donor',  (2012), ', Madras Cafe',  (2013), and ', Pik,  (2015). He also produced the 2012 film ', Aparajita Tumi', . He won the National Film Award for ', Vicky Donor',  in Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment category, shared with ', John Abraham', . He also won the Indian Competition Special Jury Award at ', u"Osian's Cinefan Festival of Asian and Arab Cinema",  in 2005 for ', Yahaan', .', [1]',  He is now making a documentary on ', Amitabh Bachchan', .', [2]',  He is associated with Rising Sun Films and has made television commercials for Saffola, 2G, Apsara Pencils, ', Fair & Lovely', , ', Maruti WagonR', , ', Cadbury', , ', Dove', , ', Zomato', , and many other brands.', 
', He is known for making independent films. ', Aparajita Tumi',  is an adapted screenplay from ', Sunil Gangopadhyay', u"'s ", Dui Naari Haathey Torobaari', .', [3]',  His 2012 film ', Vicky Donor',  was grand box office success, earning ', \u20b9',  13.40 crores in the opening weekend.', [4]', u" Sircar's ", Shoebite',  went into production in 2012 but was cancelled.', [5]', [6]', [7]',  His film, ', Madras Cafe', , was released in 2013, followed by ', Pik,  in 2015.',
