', History', [', edit', ]', 
', Jaiprakash Gaur was the founder and chairman of Jaiprakash Associates Limited. After acquiring a Diploma in Civil Engineering in 1950 from the now called ', Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee',  (IITR) in ', Roorkee', , Uttarakhand, he had a stint with the ', Government of Uttar Pradesh', u". Later he became an entrepreneur, started as a civil contractor in 1958. Now the Jaypee group is the third largest cement producer in the country. The group's cement facilities are located today all over India in 10 states, with 18 plants having an aggregate cement production capacity of 24 million tonnes and same is poised to become 36 million Tonnes before October 2011.", [4]',

', Overview', [', edit', ]', 
', u"Jaypee is India's first largest cement producer and the largest private sector ", hydropower',  company with 1,700 MW in operation. The Jaypee Group successfully completed projects in 18 states of India and ', Bhutan', u". Jaypee is the engineering and construction company for India's ", Yamuna Expressway', , which opened 9 August 2012.', 
', u"JIL, the group flagship, has an engineering and construction wing which mostly supports Jaypee projects. It also has the largest land bank in India's ", National Capital Region', , i.e., New Delhi. Jaypee has four thermal power plants totalling 5,120 MW under construction, and these are slated to go on stream by December 2014.', 
', Hydropower projects', [', edit', ]', 
', 
', Sardar Sarovar Dam', : a ', gravity dam',  on the narmada near ', Navagam', u", Gujarat; it is India's largest concrete dam", 
', Tehri Dam', : a multipurpose rock-and-earth-fill ', embankment dam',  on the ', Bhagirathi River',  near ', Tehri', u" in Uttarakhand; it is Asia's highest Rockfill dam", 
', Nathpa Jhakri Dam', : an ', Largest underground powerhouse in country',  on the ', Sutlej River',  in Himachal Pradesh', 
', Indirasagar Dam', : the second-largest surface powerhouse in India on the ', Narmada River',  at Narmadanagar in the ', Khandwa district',  of ', Madhya Pradesh', 
', Karcham Wangtoo Hydroelectric Plant',  - is a 1000MW run-of-the-river hydroelectric power station on the Sutlej River in Kinnaur district of Himachal Pradesh, India', 
', In addition, with 3200 MW projects (2700 MW Lower Siang & 500 MW Hirong ) coming up in Arunachal Pradesh and 720 MW (270 MW Umngot and 450 MW Kynshi Stage \u2013II) in Meghalaya, the Group will have total Hydropower generation capacity of over 5600 MW by 2020.',

', In September 2015, ', JSW Energy',  acquired two hydropower Projects of Jaiprakash Associates in a deal worth ', \u20b9', 9,700 crore.', [5]',

', u"On 29 February, 2016 it was reported that Jaypee group's cement assets will be acquired by ", Ultra tech',  amounting to ', \u20b9', 16,500 crore',  (US$2.5 billion) to laid off the debt, making it the largest deal in Indian cement sector.', [7]', [8]',
