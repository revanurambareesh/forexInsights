', Julien Day School',  (JDS) is a co-educational primary, secondary and senior secondary school in ', Kolkata', , ', West Bengal', , India. The school is run by "The Julien Educational Trust", a private organisation of people of the Anglo-Indian community, and is running four branches of the school at Kolkata (Elgin Road), Ganganagar, Kalyani and Howrah.The school was founded by Late Mrs. Grace Julien, and governed by the Board of Trustees of the Julien Educational Trust. The Julien Day School at Kolkata was established in 1969, Ganganagar in 1976 and Kalyani in 1988 are all affiliated to the ', Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations', u", New Delhi with I.C.S.E. at the class X level and I.S.C. at the class XII level comprising three streams viz. Science, Commerce, Humanities (Arts). The Howrah Branch was established in 2007 will also seek affiliation to the Council as the other three branches. The badge of the school bears a quote of Sir Francis Bacon '", Knowledge is power', u"'. The language of instruction of the school is strictly ", English', .', 
', There are three major vacations during the year. The Summer Vacation in May-June, the Durga Puja Vacation in September-October and the Christmas Vacation in December-January.',
