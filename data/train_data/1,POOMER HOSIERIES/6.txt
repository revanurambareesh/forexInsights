', History', [', edit', ]', 
', Rupa was established by Mr P R Agarwala, Mr G P Agarwala and Mr K B Agarwala with a vision to serve the erstwhile ', hosiery',  market, which was at a very nascent stage at that time and mainly addressed by the unorganized players. The first establishment set up by the Agarwala Brothers was called Binod ', Hosiery', . But the need for a brand name urged them to incorporate Rupa and Company Pvt. Ltd. in 1985. A decade later, it transformed into a public limited company and renamed as Rupa and Company Limited. Over the years, it has emerged as a key player in Indian Innerwear segment known for its comfort and pricing.',

', Products', [', edit', ]', 
', Rupa offers a wide range of products for both men and women including ', Vests', , ', Briefs', , ', Drawers', , ', Bermudas', , ', Capris', , ', T-shirts', , Lounge Wear, Boxer Shorts and Sleep Wear for men; and ', Bras', , ', Panties', , ', Camisoles',  and ', Leggings',  for women. Its product portfolio also includes Baba Suits, Bloomers and Slips for infants/toddlers, as well as Lounge Wear, Bermudas and Tshirts for kids. Thermal wear is another major category that Rupa caters to: the offerings include ', Winter Wear',  for both men and ', women', .', 
', Brand ambassadors', [', edit', ]', 
', Rupa is one of the first Inner Wear Companies to explore celebrity endorsements for its branding exercise. At various points in time, it has associated with various celebrities like ', Govinda', , ', Sanjay Dutt', , ', Aishwarya Rai', , ', Ronit Roy', , etc. in order to consolidate its positioning in the Inner Wear and Casual Wear segment. Presently, the Company has tied up with ', Bollywood',  heart-throb ', Ranveer Singh',  and sizzling ', Bipasha Bas,  for promoting its brands, Frontline and Softline respectively.',
