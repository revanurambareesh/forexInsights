Adani Transmission Limited is an India-based company, which is engaged in engineering, construction management or operation and maintenance business. The Company manages a portfolio, including Mundra-Mohindergarh Transmission Line, Mundra-Dehgam Transmission Line, Tiroda-Koradi-Akola-Auranagabad Transmission Network and Tiroda-Warora Transmission Line. Its EPC portfolio includes Mundra-Zerda Transmission Line, Nalia-Bitta Transmission Line, Kawai-Chhabra Transmission Line and Shantigram-Transmission Network. Its Mundra-Mohindergarh Transmission Line is of approximately plus or minus 500 kilovolts high-voltage direct current (HVDC) (over 989 kilometers).
