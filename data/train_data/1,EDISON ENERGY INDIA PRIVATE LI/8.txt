        ', Rajasthan Solar Association (RSA) is a business organization set-up under Rajasthan Non-Trading Companies Act 1960 for the ',  Promotion of solar power',  segment in Rajasthan. RSA members (45 Nos.) include  top international &  national companies (SUN EDISON, SKY POWER GLOBAL, ACME SOLAR, MAHINDRA SUSTEN, RELIANCE POWER, RENEW POWER to name a few)comprising solar project developers, EPC companies, module manufacturers, equipment manufacturers and service providers. 
          ', 
          ',  Since its inception, RSA has played a key role in shaping encouraging solar policies and suitable regulatory framework in Rajasthan, facilitating industry-academia interface, generating awareness on contemporary industry issues and promoting research in the field of solar industry. RSA works very closely with national solar federations and other industry organizations and contribute to Pan-India issues of solar sector.', 
        ', RSA also works closely with international solar associations and multilateral agencies. We are committed to promoting solar energy by advancing solar-friendly policy-making and spreading awareness. 
          One of main near term goals of the association is to mobilize Investors to meet the target of 25000 MW, set by the Rajasthan Govt. by 2021-22, as a part of National Target of 100 GW by 2021-22. ',  RSA has recently organized many mile stone events such as Rajasthan Solar Development Summit -2014 on 19 December 2014 at Jaipur, Net Metering Roadshow on 19 June 2015 at Neemrana, Net Metering Summit on 26 June 2015 at Jaipur and MSME - Energy Tech 2014 - 2015. ', 
        ', Rajasthan has installed more than 1175 MW Grid connected solar power plants out of around 4262 MW the total installed capacity of India and the State of Rajasthan has achieved highest installed Solar Power capacity and is leading in Solar Power projects allocation in the country. Rajasthan is expected to gain further prominence due to easy availability of vast land parcel, high irradiance and positive regulatory framework.   The potential of Rajasthan in solar energy is enormous and Government of Rajasthan is promoting it on a large scale. . ',
