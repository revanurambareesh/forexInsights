', u"Mehta's first feature film, ", Aaja Nachle', , was produced by ', Yash Raj Films', . Released on 9 November 2007 ', [1]',  the film stars ', Madhuri Dixit',  (in her comeback film), ', Konkona Sen Sharma', , ', Akshaye Khanna',  and ', Kunal Kapoor', .', [2]', 
', He won the ', National Film Award for Best Cinematography',  for his work in ', Hum Dil De Chuke Sanam',  (1999)',
