', Infrastructure Debt Funds', 
', In order to accelerate and enhance the flow of long term debt to infrastructure projects in India, The Union Finance Minister, in his Budget speech for 2011-12, had announced the setting up of Infrastructure Debt Funds (IDFs).', 
', The Government of India (GoI) and regulators have announced the broad structure, eligibility criteria for Sponsors, Income Tax benefits and inherent credit enhancement mechanisms. ', 
', IDFs set up as Companies under the Companies Act, would refinance eligible infrastructure projects that have completed at least 1 year of satisfactory operations. ', 
', Though refinance for infrastructure projects is an established business in India, the Infrastructure Debt Fund format is relatively new and seeks to achieve the following overall objectives:', 
', 
', Reduction in Asset Liability mismatch of banks',  
', Freeing-up of exposure of banks to Business Groups', 
', Lower cost long term refinance for projects ', 
', Increased foreign investments into India\u2019s infrastructure sectors', 
', Deepening, widening and broadening the bond market in India',

', L&T Infra Debt Fund', 
', 
', 
', 
', 
 ',   
', Structure', 
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) is an Infrastructure Debt Fund registered under the Companies Act and regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI).',  
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) was incorporated on March 19, 2013 and received the Certificate of Registration (CoR) from RBI on October 21, 2013, permitting the company to commence and carry on the business of an IDF-NBFC (Infrastructure Debt Fund \u2013 Non Banking Financial Company).', 
', Capital structure and shareholding pattern', 
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) has a share capital of Rs. 304 crore (USD 46 million).', 

', L&T Infra Finance (an Infrastructure Finance Company-IFC registered with RBI) holds 48.4% in the equity capital of L&T Infra Debt Fund Limited. (As per RBI guidelines, the Sponsor shall be a Bank or IFC and shall contribute maximum 49 % and minimum 30% to the equity capital of IDF-NBFC).', 

', Outside liabilities ', 
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) would leverage its net worth through the issue of long-tenor (5-15 year) bonds to reputed investors, including pension funds, insurance companies and other key investors - both domestic and foreign.', 
- L&T IDF has issued multi tenor(5 year/7 year/10 year/15 year) long term non convertible debentures (NCDs)in FY 2015, to reputed investors.', 

', Credit rating', 
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) has received the highest domestic credit rating of \u201cAAA\u201d from 2 accredited rating agencies, CRISIL (a Standard and Poor\u2019s Company) and CARE. This is based inter alia on the (i) well-defined regulatory & operating framework designed specifically for IDF-NBFC (ii) the domain expertise of the L&T Group in infrastructure (iii) the financing experience of L&T Financial Services and (iv) clear and focussed business model (v) experienced management. ', 

', Value proposition to clientele', 
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) offers refinancing solutions to eligible clients (infrastructure projects which have completed at least 1 year of satisfactory operations), in line with regulatory guidelines. ', 
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) offers lower cost refinance, elongated tenures and structured repayment/ redemption schedules which match the cash flow of the project. L&T IDF offers contractual and financial structuring that would
enable credit enhancement thereby improving viability and credit rating of projects. ', 


', Commencement of operations', 
', L&T Infra Debt Fund (L&T IDF) commenced business on November 21, 2013. L&T IDF is one of the first IDF-NBFCs to commence operations.
