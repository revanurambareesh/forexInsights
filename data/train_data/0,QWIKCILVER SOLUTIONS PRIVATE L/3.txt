', Qwikcilver',  is a technology company in ', India',  delivering ', gift card',  and ', stored-value card',  solutions to retail customers.', [1]',  Qwikcilver is one of the leading providers of retailer gift cards and employee reward cards in India.', [2]', [3]', [4]',  It manages the entire gift card programme for retailers including design, storage and transactions pertaining to it.', [5]',  The company also launched Woohoo.in and Woohoo App as its B2C platforms that enable consumers and corporate to purchase gift cards and vouchers of various brands.', [6]', [7]', [8]', [9]',

', Inception', [', edit', ]', 
', Qwikcilver was founded in 2008 by Kumar Sudarshan and TP Pratap.', [2]', [8]', [10]',  The company works on a ', Software-as-a-Service model (SaaS)', , enabling customers to launch the stored value card program with minimal upfront investment and provides a suite of services to create, deploy and manage the programme.', [1]', [11]',  It provides a B2B backend technology to all the transactions that happen for plastic gift cards of various brands. It powers the gift card service for brands like ', Shoppers Stop', ,', [12]',  Amazon India,', [13]',  ', Flipkart', , ', Myntra', , ', Titan',  and ', Puma', .', [3]', [14]', 
', Growth', [', edit', ]', 
', Qwikcilver powers the entire gift card business of over 100 brands across 20 categories like apparel, jewellery, dining, spa and travel among others.', [15]',  The company is estimated to have around 90% of the prepaid and gift card technology market share in India.', [4]', [9]', [16]', [17]',  It processes over 50 million transactions annually across physical and digital cards across desktop and mobile in over 300 towns and cities.', [15]', [18]', 
', Qwikcilver network has grown by 300 per cent year-on-year in the last four years.', [16]',  At present the gift card market in India is estimated at $500 million (over Rs 3,000 crore) and projected to treble over the next two years.', [15]', 
', Investors', [', edit', ]', 
', Qwikcilver initially was funded by friends and family.', [17]',  The company got the first round of undisclosed amount of funding from venture capitalist firms ', Helion Venture Partners',  and ', Accel Partners',  in 2008.', [17]',  In 2014, Qwikcilver raised $10 million in a funding round led by US-based ', e-commerce',  giant Amazon.', [19]',  The firm\u2019s existing investors Helion Venture Partners and Accel Partners also participated in the round.', [20]', 
', Recent news and activities', [', edit', ]', 
', In March 2015, Qwikcilver roped in Sanjay Tambwekar as its Chief Technology Officer (CTO).', [21]',  Tambwekar did his MS from ', North Carolina State University',  and completed management studies from ', INSEAD', , ', France', .', [21]',  He had previously worked with ', NetApp', , ', Sasken Communications',  and ', Verifone', .', [21]', 
', In August 2014, Mahesh Bhalla was appointed as its president. An alumnus of ', Institute of Management Technology (Ghaziabad)', , Bhalla was earlier executive director and general manager at ', Dell',  India\u2019s consumer & SMB division.', [22]',
