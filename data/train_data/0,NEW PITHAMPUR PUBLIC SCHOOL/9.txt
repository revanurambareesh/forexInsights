', New Digamber Public School Ground',  is ground located in the campus of New Digamber Public School, ', Indore', , ', Madhya Pradesh', . The ground was founded in 2001. The ground has got pavilion and can accommodate 5000 persons. It has basketball and volleyball courts and a five international level cricket practice nets along with one gymnasium hall. It also has facilities for swimming, table tennis, billiards,and tennis courts. It has one main clubhouse.', [1]', [2]', [3]', 
', The ground has hosted two ', List A',  matches', [4]',  when ', Rajasthan cricket team',  played against ', Vidarbha cricket team',  in 2009 ', Vijay Hazare Trophy', .', [5]',  The ground has hosted one more ', List A',  match ', Rajasthan cricket team',  played against ', Railways cricket team',  in 2009.', [6]',  Since then the ground has hosted non-first-class matches.', [7]',

The ground has got pavilion and can accommodate 5000 persons. It has basketball and volleyball courts and a five international level cricket practice nets along with one gymnasium hall.
