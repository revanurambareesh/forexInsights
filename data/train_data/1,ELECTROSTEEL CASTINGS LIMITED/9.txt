Electrosteel Castings Limited is a pipeline solution provider. The Company is engaged in the business of manufacturing Ductile Iron (DI) Pipes, Ductile Iron Fittings (DIF) and Cast Iron (CI) Pipes. It provides techno-economic solutions for water supply and sewerage systems. It is also involved in the execution of turnkey projects as an engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) contractor for infrastructure projects, including sourcing, treatment and distribution in its portfolio of services. Its range of pipes include DI socket and spigot pipe with push-on and DI flanged pipe. Its range of fittings include DI fittings with push-on type restrained joint and DI mechanical joint fittings with rubber gasket and follower gland. Its manufacturing facility at Khardah produces DIF and Pig Iron. The CI spun pipes are manufactured at the Company facility in Elavur (Tamil Nadu), while Low Ash Metallurgical Coke (LAMC) is produced at the Industrial Unit at Haldia (West Bengal).
