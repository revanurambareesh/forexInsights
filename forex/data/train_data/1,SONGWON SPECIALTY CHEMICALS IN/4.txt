', "Being located in the heart of the Asian electronics industry and our long tradition in manufacturing high quality specialty chemicals Songwon is perfectly positioned to contribute to the success of this exciting co-operation." explains Maurizio Butti, CEO, Songwon Industrial Group. ', About Heraeus',
