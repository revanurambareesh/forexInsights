', Saifai',  (', Hindi', : \u0938\u0948\u092b\u093c\u0908) is a village in the ', Etawah district', [2]',  and ', Mainpuri',  ', Parliamentary constituency',  of ', Uttar Pradesh', .', [3]',  It is a ', Tehsil',  and Block (Vikas Khand) of the ', Etawah district', . It is the birthplace of ', Mulayam Singh Yadav', , founder president of ', Samajwadi Party', , former ', Minister of Defence',  and former ', Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh', .', [4]',

', Geography', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai is situated in ', Etawah district',  at the border with ', Mainpuri district', . Area of Saifai village is 887.47 hectares and area of Saifai Block (Vikas Khand) is 20,403.30 hectares.', [5]',  It is located 23 km towards North from District headquarters and nearest city ', Etawah', . ', Mainpuri',  is located 32 km away from Saifai. It is about 110 km away from historic city of ', Taj Mahal', , ', Agra',  and 230 km from State capital ', Lucknow', .', 
', Saifai Tehsil & Block', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai is a ', Tehsil',  and Block(Vikas Khand) of ', Etawah district', , ', Uttar Pradesh', . There are about 60 villages in Saifai block.', [6]',  Saifai Block(Vikas Khand) comes under ', Jaswantnagar Vidhan Sabha constituency',  and ', Mainpuri Lok Sabha constituency', .', [3]', 
', Education', [', edit', ]', 
', University', [', edit', ]', 
', 
', Uttar Pradesh University of Medical Sciences',  (formerly ', U.P. Rural Institute of Medical Sciences and Research', ) a ', medical research',  ', public universityestablished',  by ', Government of Uttar Pradesh',  under Act,15 of 2016. The University is running full-fledged ', Medical College', , Paramedical College, Nursing College, Pharmacy College, Multi Specialty 850 bedded ', hospital',  and 150 bedded ', trauma',  and ', burn centre', . 500 bedded super specialty hospital is being established in the University by ', Government of Uttar Pradesh',  with budget of Rs. 650 crores.', [7]', [8]', [9]', 
', 
', Colleges', [', edit', ]', 
', 
', Chaudhary Charan Singh Post Graduate College', , Heonra-Saifai, ', Etawah',  or ', Chaudhary Charan Singh Degree College', , a college offering under-graduate and post-graduate courses in Science, Arts, Commerce, Computer, Management, Education and Physical Education faculties. The College is situated in Heonra village which is part of Saifai Block(Vikas Khand) and neighbour village of Saifai village.', [10]', 
', Saifai Sports College', , a residential sports ', college',  established in 2014. It offers teaching from 6th to 12th standard and sports training in ', cricket', , ', football', , ', hockey', , ', wrestling', , ', athletics', , ', badminton', , ', swimming',  and ', kabaddi', .', 
', 
', In 2015, the work of a first-of-its-kind ', Sports',  ', University',  started at Saifai.', [11]', 
', 
', S.S. Memorial Educational Academy',  offers 2 year B.T.C. which is also known as Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) outside of ', Uttar Pradesh', .', 
', Chaudhary Charan Singh College of Law', , Heonra-Saifai, ', Etawah',  is offering under-graduate course in ', Law',  faculty. The College is situated in Heonra village which is part of Saifai Block(Vikas Khand) and neighbour village of Saifai village.', [12]', [13]',

', Transport', [', edit', ]', 
', Road', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai village is connected to ', Mainpuri',  and ', Etawah',  by a four-lane ', State Highway',  (SH 83). Proposed ', Agra-Lucknow Expressway',  which is passing near to Saifai, will give it express connectivity to ', Delhi', , ', Agra', , ', Kannauj',  and ', Lucknow', .', 
', Airport', [', edit', ]', 
', Main article: ', Saifai Airport', 
', There is an ', airstrip',  situated at Saifai. This airstrip was defunct, but is being repaired during the governorship of the ', Samajwadi Party', . The airport has only unscheduled chartered flights.', 
', In 2015, ', Mirage 2000',  aircraft of ', Indian Air Force',  have been operating from the airstrip, testing grounds for possible development of this airport in case of emergency, like a war.', [15]', 
', Rail', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai Railway Station',  is situated at newly built ', Etawah', -', Mainpuri',  railway track. It connects Saifai to ', Etawah Railway Station', , which is situated at ', Howrah\u2013Delhi main line', .', [16]', 
', Census 2011', [', edit', ]', 
', Village', [', edit', ]', 
', According to ', Census 2011', , Saifai is a large village located in ', Etawah district', , ', Uttar Pradesh',  with total 1481 families residing. The Saifai village has population of 7141 of which 3917 are males while 3224 are females as per Population Census 2011.', [2]', 
', In Saifai village population of children with age 0-6 is 1059 which makes up 14.83% of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Saifai village is 823 which is lower than ', Uttar Pradesh',  state average of 912. Child Sex Ratio for the Saifai as per census is 912, higher than ', Uttar Pradesh',  average of 902.', [2]', 
', Saifai village has higher literacy rate compared to ', Uttar Pradesh', . In 2011, literacy rate of Saifai village was 82.44% compared to 67.68% of ', Uttar Pradesh', . In Saifai, Male literacy stands at 89.27% while female literacy rate was 74.00%.', [2]', 
', Block (Vikas Khand)', [', edit', ]', 
', According to ', Census 2011', , Saifai has 19,578 families residing. The Saifai Block (Vikas Khand) has population of 1,12,620 of which 60,479 are males while 52,141 are females as per Population Census 2011.', [5]', 
', Stadiums', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai is in the process of establishing itself as a sports hub in the State. Saifai has two Sports Complexes.', 
', 
', International Sports Stadium, Saifai',  with an ', athletics stadium', , an indoor stadium, an indoor ', swimming pool',  and under constriction ', international cricket stadium', .', 
', Chandgi Ram Sports Complex', , which is an international-level sports complex having a ', hockey pitch', , a ', badminton hall',  and a ', swimming pool', . ', Chandgi Ram Sports Complex',  is named after Haryanvi wrestler ', Chandgi Ram', .', 
', 
', Saifai Mahotsav', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai Mahotsav',  is an annual ', controversial',  event, which is famous for participation of ', bollywood',  ', celebrities', . It was started by Late Ranveer Singh and now it takes place in Saifai village every year in the memory of Late Ranveer Singh, who was nephew of ', Mulayam Singh Yadav',  and father of ', Tej Pratap Singh Yadav',  (MP from ', Mainpuri', ).',

It is the birthplace of ', Mulayam Singh Yadav', , founder president of ', Samajwadi Party', , former ', Minister of Defence',  and former ', Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh', .', [4]',

', Geography', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai is situated in ', Etawah district',  at the border with ', Mainpuri district', . Area of Saifai village is 887.47 hectares and area of Saifai Block (Vikas Khand) is 20,403.30 hectares.', [5]',  It is located 23 km towards North from District headquarters and nearest city ', Etawah', . ', Mainpuri',  is located 32 km away from Saifai. It is about 110 km away from historic city of ', Taj Mahal', , ', Agra',  and 230 km from State capital ', Lucknow', .', 
', Saifai Tehsil & Block', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai is a ', Tehsil',  and Block(Vikas Khand) of ', Etawah district', , ', Uttar Pradesh', . There are about 60 villages in Saifai block.', [6]',  Saifai Block(Vikas Khand) comes under ', Jaswantnagar Vidhan Sabha constituency',  and ', Mainpuri Lok Sabha constituency', .', [3]', 
', Education', [', edit', ]', 
', University', [', edit', ]', 
', 
', Uttar Pradesh University of Medical Sciences',  (formerly ', U.P. Rural Institute of Medical Sciences and Research', ) a ', medical research',  ', public universityestablished',  by ', Government of Uttar Pradesh',  under Act,15 of 2016. The University is running full-fledged ', Medical College', , Paramedical College, Nursing College, Pharmacy College, Multi Specialty 850 bedded ', hospital',  and 150 bedded ', trauma',  and ', burn centre', . 500 bedded super specialty hospital is being established in the University by ', Government of Uttar Pradesh',  with budget of Rs. 650 crores.', [7]', [8]', [9]', 
', 
', Colleges', [', edit', ]', 
', 
', Chaudhary Charan Singh Post Graduate College', , Heonra-Saifai, ', Etawah',  or ', Chaudhary Charan Singh Degree College', , a college offering under-graduate and post-graduate courses in Science, Arts, Commerce, Computer, Management, Education and Physical Education faculties. The College is situated in Heonra village which is part of Saifai Block(Vikas Khand) and neighbour village of Saifai village.', [10]', 
', Saifai Sports College', , a residential sports ', college',  established in 2014. It offers teaching from 6th to 12th standard and sports training in ', cricket', , ', football', , ', hockey', , ', wrestling', , ', athletics', , ', badminton', , ', swimming',  and ', kabaddi', .', 
', 
', In 2015, the work of a first-of-its-kind ', Sports',  ', University',  started at Saifai.', [11]', 
', 
', S.S. Memorial Educational Academy',  offers 2 year B.T.C. which is also known as Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) outside of ', Uttar Pradesh', .', 
', Chaudhary Charan Singh College of Law', , Heonra-Saifai, ', Etawah',  is offering under-graduate course in ', Law',  faculty. The College is situated in Heonra village which is part of Saifai Block(Vikas Khand) and neighbour village of Saifai village.', [12]', [13]',

', Transport', [', edit', ]', 
', Road', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai village is connected to ', Mainpuri',  and ', Etawah',  by a four-lane ', State Highway',  (SH 83). Proposed ', Agra-Lucknow Expressway',  which is passing near to Saifai, will give it express connectivity to ', Delhi', , ', Agra', , ', Kannauj',  and ', Lucknow', .', 
', Airport', [', edit', ]', 
', Main article: ', Saifai Airport', 
', There is an ', airstrip',  situated at Saifai. This airstrip was defunct, but is being repaired during the governorship of the ', Samajwadi Party', . The airport has only unscheduled chartered flights.', 
', In 2015, ', Mirage 2000',  aircraft of ', Indian Air Force',  have been operating from the airstrip, testing grounds for possible development of this airport in case of emergency, like a war.', [15]', 
', Rail', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai Railway Station',  is situated at newly built ', Etawah', -', Mainpuri',  railway track. It connects Saifai to ', Etawah Railway Station', , which is situated at ', Howrah\u2013Delhi main line', .', [16]', 
', Census 2011', [', edit', ]', 
', Village', [', edit', ]', 
', According to ', Census 2011', , Saifai is a large village located in ', Etawah district', , ', Uttar Pradesh',  with total 1481 families residing. The Saifai village has population of 7141 of which 3917 are males while 3224 are females as per Population Census 2011.', [2]', 
', In Saifai village population of children with age 0-6 is 1059 which makes up 14.83% of total population of village. Average Sex Ratio of Saifai village is 823 which is lower than ', Uttar Pradesh',  state average of 912. Child Sex Ratio for the Saifai as per census is 912, higher than ', Uttar Pradesh',  average of 902.', [2]', 
', Saifai village has higher literacy rate compared to ', Uttar Pradesh', . In 2011, literacy rate of Saifai village was 82.44% compared to 67.68% of ', Uttar Pradesh', . In Saifai, Male literacy stands at 89.27% while female literacy rate was 74.00%.', [2]', 
', Block (Vikas Khand)', [', edit', ]', 
', According to ', Census 2011', , Saifai has 19,578 families residing. The Saifai Block (Vikas Khand) has population of 1,12,620 of which 60,479 are males while 52,141 are females as per Population Census 2011.', [5]', 
', Stadiums', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai is in the process of establishing itself as a sports hub in the State. Saifai has two Sports Complexes.', 
', 
', International Sports Stadium, Saifai',  with an ', athletics stadium', , an indoor stadium, an indoor ', swimming pool',  and under constriction ', international cricket stadium', .', 
', Chandgi Ram Sports Complex', , which is an international-level sports complex having a ', hockey pitch', , a ', badminton hall',  and a ', swimming pool', . ', Chandgi Ram Sports Complex',  is named after Haryanvi wrestler ', Chandgi Ram', .', 
', 
', Saifai Mahotsav', [', edit', ]', 
', Saifai Mahotsav',  is an annual ', controversial',  event, which is famous for participation of ', bollywood',  ', celebrities', . It was started by Late Ranveer Singh and now it takes place in Saifai village every year in the memory of Late Ranveer Singh, who was nephew of ', Mulayam Singh Yadav',  and father of ', Tej Pratap Singh Yadav',  (MP from ', Mainpuri', ).',
