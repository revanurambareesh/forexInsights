The system, used for wound-care dressings in heat-sealed paper packaging pouches, provides automated vision control of product position within that packaging to minimize trapped in-seal faults, along with fully automated rejection of any defective product. It was recognized for \u201csuccessful execution of a complex and complicated application, using a mix of camera, lighting and software,\u201d PPMA judges said.  ', 
',
