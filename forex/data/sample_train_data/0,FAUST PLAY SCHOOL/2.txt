Even though there are are no words and I am not singing, there are no lyrics, [Liszt] represents everything in a wonderful way\u2026It transports me to the scene and at the same time it creates something new.\u201d', A passionate admirer of Liszt, Dossin serves as vice president of the American Liszt Society and president of its Oregon Chapter.', Learn more about professor Dossin: ', https://music.uoregon.edu/people/facu...', Learn more about the University of Oregon School of Music and Dance: ', https://music.uoregon.ed,   ',
