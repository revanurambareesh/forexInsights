', Notable events and achievements', [', edit', ]', 
', Indian Model United Nations', [', edit', ]', 
', The Ryan Group of Institution has been organizing the Indian Model United Nations, a conference that brings young minds together to simulate the United Nations and its various committees.', 
', The event is held annually and witnesses students from all over the globe.', 
', u"World Scholar's Cup achievements", [', edit', ]', 
', u"Three students represented the Ryan Group in Singapore for the World Scholar's Cup, 2013 and became the first from India to qualify for the Global Round in Bangkok. One of them achieved the top spot in Writing Skills and also received the 'Top Scorer Award'. In Bangkok, one of them achieved the 'Top Scorer Award' and another achieved the third position in Collaborative Writing and through teamwork subsequently qualified for the Tournament of Champions in ", Yale University', , USA.', 
', International Children\u2019s Festival of Performing Arts', [', edit', ]', 
', Ryan International Group of School holds yearly International Children\u2019s Festival of Performing Arts which give students a relief from classroom activity and also provides them a creative escape by launching them into the imaginative world of theatre music and dance.', [11]',  The 14th edition of the event held in January 2016 in Mumbai with the theme \u2018Celebrating Diversity\u2019. The event attracted over 40000 students including notable individuals from India and 17 different countries which include ', Bangladesh', , ', Denmark', , ', Czech Republic', , ', Egypt', , ', Estonia', , ', Finland', , ', Germany', , ', Iceland', , India, ', Iran', , ', Latvia', , ', Morocco', , ', Nepal', , ', Russia', , ', Slovenia', , ', Thailand', , ', Sri Lanka', , and ', Zambia', .', [12]', [13]', 
', u"The 13th International Children's Festival of Performing Arts by Ryan International Schools was earlier held in 2015 at different venues in Delhi. Performances focusing on social issues like women empowerment, violence against women were initiated by students. Students from countries like ", Spain', , Egypt, Iran, Sri Lanka, Germany and others participated in the festival.', [14]', 
', Adoption of Tata ClassEdge way of teaching and learning', [', edit', ]', 
', Ryan International Group has joined the fraternity of schools which have adopted the ', Tata ClassEdge',  way of teaching and learning. The group has implemented the technology in its schools in ', Sharjah',  and ', Abu Dhabi', .', [15]', 
', Environment Week celebrations', [', edit', ]', 
', Environment Week was celebrated at Ryan International School, Vasant Kunj. Tree plantation drives, awareness rallies and interactive sessions were conducted at Gole Market Sports Complex & Guru Nanak Stadium in Ludhiana and Ludhiana railway station.', [16]', [17]',  Students of Ryan International School, Kundalahalli, Bengaluru, participated in various activities held as part of Environment Week celebrations. Craft activities focusing on \u2018Green India, Clean India\u2019 were carried out as a part of the celebrations.', [18]',
