MAGISTRATE/COLLECTOR', 

', District is the main unit of the state.  The District
Magistrate/Collector is the administrator of the district  . The most  important
work of the the district magistrate is to maintain law and order, various rules and
implementation of various govt. orders. Being a top administrator of the district  he
directly order the police department whenever required.  He is also responsible to
maintain peace and justice in the district.',

the District Supply Officer. ',
