Morganite Crucible (India) Limited is engaged in manufacturing and selling of silicon carbide and clay graphite crucibles and its accessories. The Company's geographical segments include India and Outside India. The Company's Outside India segment includes Asia and Far East, Europe, Africa and Middle East, North America and others. The Company is involved in product development, such as induction crucible, higher capacity crucibles, aluminum scrap melting crucibles and degassing rotors. The Company is also engaged in the development of Ultramelt mix for high erosion resistance crucibles. The Company's plant is located at B-11, MIDC Waluj in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. The Company's subsidiary, Diamond Crucible Company Limited (DCCL), has its manufacturing facility at Mehsana, Gujarat.
