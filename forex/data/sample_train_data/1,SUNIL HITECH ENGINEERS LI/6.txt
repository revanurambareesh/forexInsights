Sunil Hitech Engineers Limited is engaged in the business of engineering, procurement, construction (EPC), fabrication, erection, overhauling, maintenance, trading and other related activities. The Company is engaged in construction of utility projects. Its segments include Project, Manufacturing, Power and Project Supply.
