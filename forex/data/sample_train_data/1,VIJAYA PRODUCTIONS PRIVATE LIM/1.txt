', History', [', edit', ]', 
', Being once considered as the largest film studio in ', Asia', . The studio was a result of a merger between ', Vauhini Studios',  and ', Vijaya Productions', , when in 1948 a successful Telugu & Tamil movie producer ', Moola Narayana Swamy',  leased the studios which was under heavy liabilities to the Vijaya Productions run by ', Bommireddy Nagi Reddy', , ', Aluri Chakrapani',  who were one time partners of ', Moola Narayana Swamy',  in 1930s.', 
', Productions', [', edit', ]', 
', Some of these films such as Pathala Bhairavi, ', Pelli Chesi Chood, u" (as Kalyanam Panni Paar 6 months later), Chandraharam, and Maya Bazar were dubbed into Tamil at the same time, Missamma was remade as Missiamma (1955) with Gemini Ganesan playing NTR's role. Excepting Chandraharam and Uma Chandi Gauri Shankarula Katha. Nearly every movie made by Vijaya Productions was successful at the box office.",
